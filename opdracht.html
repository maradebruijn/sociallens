CHALLENGE WEEK 3

This weeks challenge is all about getting to know your co-students, by taking pictures that require a little small-talk first.

1. Take a picture of two students who were born in the same year.
2. Take a picture of a student with the same favorite color as you.
3. Not really a photgraphy challenge, but more of a social one: ask a student to send you a picture of their pet.
